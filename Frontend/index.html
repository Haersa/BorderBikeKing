Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a bike?

Renting a bike is easy! Simply visit our website, select your preferred bike and rental duration, and proceed to checkout. You can also visit our store to rent a bike in person.

Do you offer bike maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive bike maintenance and repair services. Whether you need a tune-up, a flat tire fixed, or major repairs, our experienced mechanics are here to help.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our priority. We regularly inspect and maintain our bikes, provide safety equipment such as helmets, and offer guidance on safe riding practices. Additionally, we sanitize our equipment regularly.

Can I cancel my bike rental reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your bike rental reservation. Please refer to our cancellation policy for details on cancellations and refunds.
