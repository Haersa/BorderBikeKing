Custom Bike Builds

Our custom bike builds service allows you to design and create your dream bike. From selecting the frame to choosing the components, our experts will work with you to ensure your bike is tailored to your needs and preferences.

Whether you're looking for a lightweight road bike for long-distance rides or a durable mountain bike for off-road adventures, we have the expertise and resources to bring your vision to life.

With our custom bike builds, you can ride with confidence knowing that your bike is optimized for performance, comfort, and style.

Repairs

Our repair services are designed to keep your bike in top condition and ensure you can enjoy smooth, trouble-free rides. Whether your bike needs a simple tune-up or major repairs, our skilled technicians are here to help.

We offer a comprehensive range of repair services, including fixing flat tires, adjusting brakes and gears, replacing worn-out components, and more. Our goal is to get your bike back on the road quickly and efficiently so you can get back to riding.

At Bike King Borders, we use high-quality parts and equipment to ensure lasting repairs and optimal performance. You can trust us to provide honest advice, reliable service, and affordable pricing.

Guided Tours

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with our guided bike tours. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a beginner, our guided tours offer a unique opportunity to explore new places, meet like-minded riders, and experience the thrill of discovery.

Our experienced guides will lead you on carefully planned routes that showcase the beauty of the Scottish Borders. From scenic countryside roads to challenging mountain trails, our tours cater to riders of all skill levels and interests.

Join us for a day of exploration, camaraderie, and fun as we pedal through picturesque landscapes, visit historic landmarks, and discover hidden gems off the beaten path. Book your guided tour today and let the adventure begin!
